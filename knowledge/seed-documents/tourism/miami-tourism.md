After a match, favor areas with realistic return transport and late-evening food or hotel access. For Miami, useful pairing districts include Wynwood, Brickell, Downtown Miami, Miami Beach, Little Havana; the constraint is: Fans should choose return transport before leaving for Miami Gardens, especially from beach or Brickell hotels.
